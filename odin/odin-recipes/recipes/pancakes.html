Pancakes

Description

Fluffy, golden pancakes perfect for a hearty breakfast. Serve with syrup and fruits.

Simple to make and loved by all ages.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk in eggs, milk, and melted butter.
  3. Heat a pan and pour batter to form pancakes.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook the other side.
  5. Serve hot with toppings of your choice.
