Salad

Description

This fresh salad is healthy and quick to prepare. Ideal for a light lunch or side dish.

Customizable with your favorite veggies and dressing.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. Mix them in a large bowl.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Toss well and serve immediately.
