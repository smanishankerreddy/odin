Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna is rich, cheesy, and packed with flavor. It's a perfect family dinner!

The layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese make it a satisfying comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Brown the beef in a skillet, then add tomato sauce.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat layers and finish with mozzarella and parmesan on top.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes until bubbly and golden brown.
  7. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
